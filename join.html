RECRUITMENT
  1. APPLICATION - We conduct recruitment twice a year, at the beginning of every semester. Returning members do not have to fill out the written application but will be required to interview. The Fall 2017 application can be accessed here and is due on September 1, 2017. 
  2. INTERVIEW - If selected to interview, you will meet with members of the executive board to answer behavioral questions, to elaborate on your interest in joining VCG, and to conduct a basic case interview. Interviews typically last between 10-15 minutes. Interviews will take place between September 3 - September 6 from 11 AM - 5 PM at the Starbucks on the Corner. 
  3. MEMBERSHIP INVITATION - The weekend after interviews complete, a final decision is delivered to your email address. We either extend a membership invitation, a waitlist notification, or advice on how to improve your application for the next recruitment cycle. Potential members typically have 2 days to respond to our offer before we extend invitations to our waitlist candidates.
